How does payment work?+

We use Paystack, Nigeria's leading payment gateway. You can pay with any Nigerian debit/credit card, USSD, or bank transfer. No dollar card required - everything is billed in Naira.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. There are no lock-in contracts. Cancel anytime from your account dashboard and you retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Is my data secure?+

We do not store your AI conversations on our servers. All communication is encrypted end-to-end. Payment data is handled entirely by Paystack's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure - we never see your card details.

Do I need a dollar card or foreign account?+

Not at all. UsenPrime was built specifically for Nigerians. Pay with your regular Nigerian bank card, USSD code, or bank transfer. Everything runs in Naira.

What happens if I miss a payment?+

Your subscription will pause at the end of the current billing cycle. Your account and history are kept safe. Simply renew your subscription whenever you're ready and access is restored instantly.

Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?+

Yes. You can upgrade at any time. Just subscribe to the Pro plan and your access will be upgraded immediately. Contact our support team and we'll help sort out any proration.

How many devices can I use?+

Your subscription works across all your devices - phone, tablet, and desktop. Log in from any browser with no limits on the number of devices connected to your account.
